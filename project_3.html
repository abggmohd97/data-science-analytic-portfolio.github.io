FIFA 22: Data Exploration and Analysis

PROJECT: Data Analytic

The Scenario

The next progression of our project, involving an exciting topic, generated our own point of interest. For this time we will develop and create a data investigation, exploration, and analysis on one of my favourite video game series of all time, EA SPORTS FIFA 22. EA SPORTS FIFA is a football simulation video game that has existed for a long time since 1994 and a major gaming series in football video games world, playable in various gaming platform since then. Throughout this video game, every individual criterion involved such as footballers, coaches, clubs, nationality, tournaments, rules, and format is based on the actual football event in the world right now. By developing a detailed and accurate analysis on this topic, we can see clearly obtain and see for ourself how each of the data created various of unique and interesting trends, allowing us to create a new conclusion for that particular understanding

The Data

All information about the data can be found by viewing the dataset used. In summary, Fifa 22 is a football simulation video game published by Electronic Arts, It is the 29th installment in the FIFA series, and was released worldwide on 1 October 2021 for Microsoft Windows, Nintendo Switch, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Xbox One and Xbox Series X/S gaming platform. The data used for this project has been made available in specific data set: FIFA 22 Complete Player Dataset (CC0: Public Domain, dataset made available by Stefano Leone) from Kaggle. The datasets provided include the players data for the Career Mode from FIFA 15 to FIFA 22, including players URL of the uploaded player faces, club and nation logos, player positions, player attributes and player personal data like nationality, club, date of birth, wage, salary and etc.
